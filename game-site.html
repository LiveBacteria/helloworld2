Hearts of Aurorus is a 2D pixel-platformer, inspired by the early Contra™ and Metroid™ series. Our team is currently in the preliminary stages of creating the demo version. Art assets and storyboards are actively being created.

A download for the game is not yet available. We will be releasing an alpha-ish demo by the year end, 2018.
